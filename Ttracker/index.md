# Privacy Policy for Tuition Tracker

**Last Updated:** January 13, 2025

## Overview

Tuition Tracker is a mobile application designed to help tutors track their tuition classes. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

## Data We Collect

### Data Stored Locally

- Student names, phone numbers, and locations
- Class schedules and notes
- App preferences and settings

**All this data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.**

## Third-Party Services

### Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

- Device identifiers
- IP address
- General location (country/region)
- App usage data

For more information, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## Data Sharing

We do not collect, store, or share your personal data. All tuition data remains on your device.

## Data Security

Your data is stored only on your device using standard Android storage mechanisms.

## Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date.
