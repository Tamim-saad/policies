# Privacy Policy for Tuition Tracker

**Last Updated:** January 21, 2025

## Overview

Tuition Tracker is a mobile application designed to help tutors and students manage and track their tuition classes. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

## Data We Collect

### Data Stored Locally

- Student names, phone numbers, and locations
- Class schedules and notes
- App preferences and settings

**All this data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.**

## Third-Party Services

### Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in our app. AdMob may collect and use certain information for advertising purposes. This may include:

- Device identifiers (e.g., advertising ID)
- IP address
- General location (country/region)
- App usage data and interaction with ads
- Device information (model, OS version)

For more information about how Google uses data, please visit: [How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites)

For Google's Privacy Policy, please visit: [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### Your Choices Regarding Advertising

You can opt-out of personalized advertising by:

1. Going to your device's Settings > Google > Ads
2. Enabling "Opt out of Ads Personalization"

When you opt out, you will still see ads, but they will be less relevant to you.

## Data Sharing

We do not collect, store, or share your personal data on our servers. All tuition data remains on your device.

The only data shared is through Google AdMob for advertising purposes as described above.

## Data Security

Your data is stored only on your device using standard Android storage mechanisms. We do not have access to your data.

## Children's Privacy

**Important:** This app is intended for tutors (adults) to manage their tuition classes. It is NOT intended for children under 13 to use directly.

If tutors use this app to track tutoring sessions with students under 13, please note:

- The student data is stored locally on the tutor's device only
- We do not collect or transmit any student information to our servers
- Advertisements shown in the app are intended for the tutor (adult user), not for students

We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## User Consent for Advertising

By using this app, you acknowledge that:

- Advertisements may be displayed by Google AdMob
- Google may collect and use data as described in their privacy policy
- You can opt-out of personalized advertising through your device settings

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), UK, or other regions with similar regulations, the app will request your consent before showing personalized advertisements.

## Data Retention and Deletion

All your tuition data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all data at any time by:

1. Going to Settings in the app
2. Selecting "Clear All Data"

Alternatively, uninstalling the app will remove all associated data.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this privacy policy periodically.
